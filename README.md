# NSA Policy Hub

**NSA Policy Hub** is a personal policy research, data analysis, and knowledge-management website developed by **Nusanta Samayel Audri**.

The platform serves as a central index for my research, policy writing, data projects, statistical analysis, advocacy materials, program outputs, and curated resource collections across public policy, social protection, health equity, climate vulnerability, governance, youth policy, economic development, and applied statistics.

---

## Purpose of the Website

This website is designed to organize and present my academic, policy, and analytical work in one accessible place. It brings together selected research outputs, policy analysis, data-driven projects, writing portfolios, and thematic resource collections.

The goal is to make my work easier to navigate, update, and share with collaborators, mentors, reviewers, faculty, and policy-oriented audiences.

My work combines academic research, policy analysis, program coordination, and youth-focused advocacy.
