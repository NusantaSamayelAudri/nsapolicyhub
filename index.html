About

I am a policy researcher with experience in mixed-methods research, R-based data analysis, survey design, qualitative fieldwork, policy writing, program evaluation, and youth-focused policy engagement. My work connects social protection, climate vulnerability, health equity, governance, economic development, and institutional access.

This website organizes my research outputs, data-analysis repositories, policy writings, project documentation, and selected advocacy or knowledge-management work in one accessible place.

Featured Research

Safety Nets and Climate-Vulnerable Women’s Health in Bangladesh

Mixed-methods study of climate-displaced women living in Dhaka informal settlements. The project examines how social safety nets, clinic access, displacement pathways, and structural barriers shape women’s health and livelihood outcomes after internal migration.

The quantitative workflow includes primary survey data cleaning, variable construction, hypothesis-based tests, logistic regression modeling, odds-ratio visualization, and figure generation in R.
